# Re-walk docs/INSTALL.md as a true non-author UAT

## Context

Phase 20 (v1.5 Legibility milestone) shipped `docs/INSTALL.md` as the canonical operator-workstation install path. Per CONTEXT.md D-02, the milestone gate was supposed to be a non-author UAT — i.e. a walker who did not participate in writing the doc would follow it verbatim and surface comprehension gaps the author cannot see.

At the time of Phase 20 close, no non-author was available. The author (cnoellert) walked the doc themselves with the explicit deviation that they were NOT a non-author of the planning artifacts (they orchestrated the phase) but WERE a non-author of the install procedure itself (the doc text was generated by Claude from codebase inference; cnoellert had never physically performed the install). The walk was conducted under a discipline contract (verbatim copy-paste, verbatim observation transcription, no silent routing-around). Outcome: PASS-with-deviations, with Deviation 0 pre-recording the author-walk.

This seed exists so the formal non-author UAT eventually happens.

## Trigger conditions

Surface this seed when ANY of the following becomes true:

- A second operator joins the project who has NOT previously deployed forge-bridge
- A consulting / contract operator is brought in for any work that involves spinning up a new forge-bridge install
- A future phase requires onboarding new team members through the install flow
- v1.6 milestone planning starts (regardless of whether a non-author is available yet — surface the seed for visibility)

## What to do when triggered

1. Hand `docs/INSTALL.md` and a fresh `20-HUMAN-UAT.md` scaffold (or equivalent for the then-current milestone) to the non-author. Do NOT give them the .planning/ artifacts — they must have only the doc.
2. Have them follow the same pattern recorded in `.planning/phases/20-reality-audit-canonical-install/20-HUMAN-UAT.md` — verbatim walk, gap log, per-surface reachability table, `forge doctor` output, deviations, outcome, sign-off.
3. Compare their gap log against the author-walk record. The deltas are the comprehension gaps that the author walk failed to surface — those are the things to patch.
4. Patch INSTALL.md per D-04 dispositions for any new gaps.
5. Update PROJECT.md / ROADMAP.md to remove the "partial gate" annotation and record full non-author UAT pass.
6. Mark this seed `status: resolved`.

## Why this isn't urgent

The author walk caught real things (the discipline contract was honored). PROJECT.md / ROADMAP.md transparently record the partial-gate status. INSTALL.md is good enough to ship to v1.5 because it walks cleanly for the author, who is — by the spirit of D-02 — a comprehension non-author of the install procedure even if not a non-author of the doc text.

But the gate isn't fully closed. This seed keeps the integrity of D-02 alive without blocking v1.5 ship.
