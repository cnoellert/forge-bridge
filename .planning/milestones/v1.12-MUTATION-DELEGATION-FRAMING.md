**Opens:** after v1.11 Source-of-Truth Routing (SR.1 closed).
**Companion ADR:** `.planning/adr/ADR-0001-chat-mutation-executor-delegation.md` (bridge half;
defers to forge-pipeline ADR-003, Door C / variant C1 — Accepted).

---

## Thesis

v1.10 Authority Invariance made operator authority + execution eligibility **routing-invariant** — the
system knows which tools mutate and acts on it consistently (DI.1 gate), and arbitrates a single tool
without paralysis (DI.2). But chat mutations still don't *land*: a mutating intent compiles to a **bare**
host-mutation step that the DI.1 gate correctly hard-blocks pre-ratify. The mutation has authority
machinery but **no ratify path to flow through**.

v1.12 closes that: chat mutations reach the **operator-ratified, executor-delegated apply path** — the
v1.7 `AssentRecord` ratify chain, with the host mutation performed by a consumer-owned
manifest-participating executor behind it (ADR-003 Door C). This completes the authority arc v1.10
opened: not just *"authority doesn't change with route"* but *"a ratified mutation actually executes."*

---

## Phases

- **C2 — Compile → Commit-Bearing Executor Chain** (phase 1, framed).
  `compile_intent()` emits `<executor> -> commit` for mutating intents so they reach
  preview/`AssentRecord`/ratify instead of the DI.1-blocked direct dispatch. Ships **intent-ratification
  (Shape A+)**: operator assents to the mutation capability + intent; the resolved `count` is surfaced at
  apply. The literal ADR-0001 deliverable — and, after grounding the executor contract, a *small*
  transform (pure name-swap + commit-append, no arg reshaping, no `mode` token).
  See `.planning/phases/C2-compile-commit-bearing-executor-chain/C2-FRAMING.md`.

- **Shape B — Manifest-Ratification** (phase 2, planned; not yet framed).
  *"Upgrade chat mutation from intent-ratification to manifest-ratification."* Runs the executor's
  discover at **preview** time, persists the resolved `MutationManifest` in the `AssentRecord`, shows it
  pre-assent, and drift-checks it across the **T_preview → T_apply** window (Window 2). A maturation of
  the ratification model, deliberately **not** smuggled into C2 (substrate-before-consumer /
  name-the-maturation-condition). Maturation condition: the room judges Window-2 drift unacceptable in
  production (e.g. a real silent-count-drift incident).
