## Architectural Finding

25.3's select primitive is working as specified. The live probe
clarified that shorthand shot identity (`genesis_0020`) and segment
identity (`genesis_0020_source_L01`) are different identity surfaces in
the active dry_run manifest.

Future options, explicitly deferred:

- field-qualified select syntax, e.g. `select shot_name genesis_0020`
- emitter-declared identity fields in the typed-port phase
- upstream manifest identity normalization

None belong in 25.3. Select remains exact identity resolution, not
retrieval.
