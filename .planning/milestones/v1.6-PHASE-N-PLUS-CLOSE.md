## Methodology promoted to memory

Four patterns cleared the promotion bar on cross-arc, distinct-shape
recurrence:

**Stage 1b as a named code-handoff spec review.** Code-handoff specs
receive a named two-stage review: Stage 1a (architectural / fix-shape)
and Stage 1b (review of the spec artifact itself against the actual
files). Across the arc Stage 1b caught structurally different classes
of instability — internal contradiction within a spec, a silently
dropped sub-lock attribution, an inert verification probe, and a
grounding-convention violation. The diversity of catch shapes, not the
repetition, is what made it memory-grade.

**Mock as stub vs. contract-enforcer vs. runtime-environment.** A
three-tier taxonomy. A stub constrains call shape only; a
contract-enforcer enforces the real execution-boundary contract; a
runtime-environment mock executes generated code against a faithful
environment. Finding A demonstrated the cost of confusing the first two
concretely — a permissive mock preserved call-shape while bypassing the
boundary contract. The tier is chosen by what the test must constrain.

**Substrate-before-consumer landing.** A substrate primitive lands and
is tested first; consumer adoption rides a separate commit. Validated
three times across three sessions and three substrate kinds — typed
ports, the mutation manifest contract, the commit primitive. This is
landing-order discipline: operational, about commit sequencing.

**Substrate-coherence-revealed-in-retrospect.** Prior substrate
decisions become load-bearing for later consumers in ways invisible
until the consumer arrives. This is epistemic — about when architectural
truth becomes visible, not about when code lands. It is a sibling of
substrate-before-consumer landing but a distinct lesson. The Phase N+
framing doc's own "Architectural Archaeology" section anticipated this
promotion, noting a third instance would make it a candidate memory; the
cycle's count (nine-plus instances across Phase 25.0, Phase N, and
Phase N+) cleared that self-stated gate.

Two further patterns promoted on **dense single-arc recurrence across
genuinely distinct pressure surfaces**: sibling-check-before-fix-scope-
declared (sibling surfaces appeared as topology references, an N-site
helper pattern, identity-resolution keys, and renderer call sites) and
the grep-c completion invariant (now carrying both a spec-side
completeness assertion and a verification-side minimality assertion).
One existing memory was extended — ground-specs-in-actual-files gained
a fourth application class, canonical-probe-text at framing-archaeology
grain — and one existing memory's promotion bar was deliberately
amended (see process refinements, below).

## Constitutional note — drift-guard as determinism enforcement

The commit primitive's verify mechanism re-dispatches the tool in
verify mode and structurally compares the fresh resolved plan against
the held plan, raising `PLAN_STATE_DRIFT` on mismatch. Its nominal
design purpose was detecting preview/apply divergence — state drift
between the moment a plan is previewed and the moment it is applied.

Observed reality across the arc: the same mechanism caught a broader
class. Three of the four observed catches were emergent — the mechanism
detected replay non-determinism, pseudo-identity instability, and
hidden statefulness, none of which were its design target. The
architecture discovered something stronger than its design intent, and
discovered it empirically rather than aspirationally.

The operator named this property:
**drift-guard-as-determinism-enforcement**. And on the arc as a whole:
**the seam held every time it mattered.** Both phrases are preserved
here verbatim as the constitutional reading of the Phase N+ arc. The C4
evidence artifact (`v1.6-PHASE-N-PLUS-C4-PROBE.md`) carries the three findings as
evidence; this section carries what they meant. Evidence artifacts
remain evidentiary; the close doc carries the synthesis.

## The promotion bar, stated

The cycle ratified an explicit promotion bar so future cycles inherit a
stated rule rather than re-litigating it:

> Cross-arc recurrence remains the preferred promotion path. Dense
> single-arc recurrence across genuinely distinct pressure surfaces
> also qualifies. The criterion is whether the substrate repeatedly
> forced the same truth into view under different forms of pressure —
> architectural truth density, not calendar distance. The room
> protects this bar deliberately: once a system mutates reality,
> coordination failures become architectural failures, and noisy
> memory becomes noisy constitutional guidance.
