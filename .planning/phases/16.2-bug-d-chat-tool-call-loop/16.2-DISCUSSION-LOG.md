**User's choice:** Bottom-up, test-first (D-01..D-04).
**Notes:** Strong reco lead with rationale that the symptom (loop terminates after one Ollama round-trip with JSON as terminal content) narrows to the adapter or below. The router/handler/UI loci are unlikely. Starting at the lowest layer gets the cheapest definitive answer. The hypothesis (D-03) is that `OllamaToolAdapter.parse_response()` reads `message.get("tool_calls") or []` and qwen2.5-coder is emitting tool calls as JSON-shaped *text* in `message["content"]` instead of the structured field — must be verified with the captured fixture before locking the fix.
